How to Play

You're a USYD postgrad CS student. You have 13 study weeks + 2 exam weeks. Each weekday morning you allocate 8 hours across studying (per course), part-time work (per job), rest, and food. Random events fire daily.

4 ways to die instantly:

  • Health drops to 0
  • Money goes below -$1000
  • 20+ uses of stimulants (addiction)
  • Win the lottery (special hidden ending)

5 ways to graduate:

  • Honours — average exam ≥ 85, healthy
  • Work King — max skill ≥ 90, all exams pass
  • PhD scholarship — 30+ days as RA, average ≥ 80, professor's favour
  • Plain pass — all 4 exams ≥ 50

1 way to fail:

  • Any exam < 50 → withdrawn
